About Us

Wild Rescues is a registered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation
facility, located in Ocala, Florida. we help injured, sick,
neglected, and orphaned wildlife. We help rehabilitate these animals
and then release them into the wild, if possible.

Wild Rescues is a member of the National Wildlife Rehabilitators Association.





We help many animals, such as:

  • Racoons
  • Squirells
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles


Did you find an injured or orphaned animal? Contact us to see if we can help.
